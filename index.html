How It Works

  • 📋 Register your team on the Teams tab — takes 30 seconds
  • 📅 Pick your time blocks on the Schedule tab so other teams know when you're free
  • 🤝 Find a team that shares your slot, shake hands, and get to the boards
  • 🌽 Show up and play — games are 20 minutes or first to 21+
  • 📱 Report your scores on the Scores tab right after your game
  • 🏆 Check the Standings tab to see where you stack up

Game Rules

  • First team to reach 21 or more points wins
  • 20-minute time limit — highest score wins if time runs out
  • Bag in the hole = 3 points
  • Bag on the board = 1 point
  • Cancellation scoring — only the net difference is counted each round
  • Bags that bounce off the ground don't count
  • Boards set 27 feet apart

Tournament Format

  • Self-scheduled — find a team, pick a block, play
  • Minimum 2 games to qualify for prizes
  • Maximum 4 games per team
  • Both teams enter scores on the app immediately after each game
  • Teams ranked by Glicko-2 rating (accounts for opponent strength, not just wins)
  • Tiebreaker: team with more games played wins
